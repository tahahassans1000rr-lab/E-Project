Our Story

ABOUT INFOCOM

Who We Are

INFOCOM DIGITAL

Infocom is a leading digital media company specializing in sports content, information portals, and interactive web experiences. Founded in 2001, we have grown into a trusted name in online sports journalism and data.

Our portfolio includes SportsInfo, GameScore, AthleteVault, and RecordBreak — all dedicated to bringing fans closer to the sports they love through cutting-edge technology and rich storytelling.

MISSION

Empower fans with accurate, engaging, and accessible sports information worldwide.

VISION

To be the world's most trusted and immersive sports information platform.

TECHNOLOGY

Modern web stack: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap, GSAP, AOS, Three.js.

REACH

Serving sports fans across 80+ countries with localised content.

Our Journey

TIMELINE

2001

Infocom founded in Karachi, Pakistan as a digital media startup.

2005

Launched first sports portal attracting 10,000 daily visitors.

2010

Expanded to cover 6 major sports disciplines with live score integration.

2016

Went global — partnered with FIFA, ITF, and FIVB for official data.

2020

Rebuilt platform with modern stack reaching 500K monthly users.

2024

Launched SportsInfo 3.0 — our most advanced portal to date.
